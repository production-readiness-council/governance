# Phase 1 Decisions

Date: July 18, 2026

## Phase 1 outputs

This repository publishes the first explicit governance foundation for the Production Readiness Council.

The following decisions are now documented:

- the governing bodies and their authority boundaries
- the report lifecycle gates from proposal through re-review
- the sponsor non-interference rule
- the contributor role criteria for Reviewer, Maintainer, and Council Member
- the default system-of-record split between GitHub and the application

## Notes

These artifacts are initial governance documents and may evolve through documented governance change.
