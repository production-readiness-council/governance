# Sponsor Non-Interference Rule

## Rule

Sponsors and funding relationships must not control technical conclusions, reviewer selection, conflict handling, or publication decisions.

## Operational meaning

Sponsors must not:

- direct or veto verdict outcomes
- choose the final review panel for a report they materially affect
- require favorable framing as a condition of support
- suppress negative findings or publication delays outside the documented workflow
- bypass GitHub or governance records for substantive technical decisions

## Allowed sponsor interactions

Sponsors may:

- suggest topics for consideration
- provide factual corrections supported by evidence
- provide funding or operational support without governance authority
- comment publicly through the same documented contribution channels as other participants

## Required handling

- Material sponsor relationships should be disclosed when relevant to a report or review.
- Conflicted participants should not act as the sole approval authority on affected reports.
- Any exception process must be public, documented, and approved through governance change, not handled ad hoc.
