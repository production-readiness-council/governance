# Contributor Role Criteria

## General principle

Role progression should be based on visible contribution proof-of-work, review quality, process reliability, and trust earned over time.

## Reviewer

A contributor may be considered for Reviewer when they have:

- a visible record of relevant technical contributions or evidence review
- demonstrated ability to distinguish evidence from assertion
- followed contribution and conduct rules consistently
- disclosed conflicts when relevant
- shown they can review rigorously without overstating certainty

Expected Reviewer responsibilities:

- provide substantive review comments on drafts or evidence
- identify unsupported claims and methodology gaps
- record conflicts affecting their review scope

## Maintainer

A contributor may be considered for Maintainer when they have:

- sustained contribution history across governance, workflow, or report operations
- demonstrated reliability in process enforcement and repository hygiene
- shown sound judgment in handling contribution boundaries and public artifacts
- earned trust to manage workflow without claiming unauthorized technical authority

Expected Maintainer responsibilities:

- maintain repository process quality
- enforce templates, traceability, and contribution norms
- help route work without deciding report conclusions alone

## Council Member

A contributor may be considered for Council Member when they have:

- a sustained record of high-quality technical and governance contribution
- demonstrated independence of judgment
- visible participation in review rigor, conflict handling, and publication decisions
- trust from existing governance participants to exercise publication authority responsibly

Expected Council Member responsibilities:

- make or confirm publication decisions within the documented governance model
- interpret governance rules in edge cases
- protect independence between funding, administration, and technical conclusions

## Promotion process

Role changes should be supported by a documented decision record that cites contribution history, rationale, and decision authority.
